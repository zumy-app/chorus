About Chorus

We're on a mission to make global communication effortless by breaking down language barriers through technology.

Our Mission

At Chorus, we believe language should never be a barrier to human connection. In an increasingly connected world, we're building technology that enables anyone to communicate naturally in their native language while being understood by everyone.

Our platform combines real-time translation with intuitive messaging, creating seamless conversations across cultures and continents. Whether you're making friends abroad, conducting international business, or learning a new language, Chorus makes it effortless.

We're not just translating words—we're building bridges between people, fostering understanding, and creating opportunities for collaboration on a global scale.

Our Story

Chorus was born from a simple observation: billions of people around the world want to connect, but language barriers hold them back. Traditional translation tools are clunky, interrupt conversations, and feel unnatural.

We asked ourselves: what if translation was invisible? What if you could chat naturally in your language while the other person reads in theirs, seamlessly and in real-time?

"Language should connect us, not divide us."

That vision led to Chorus. Built with modern technology, powered by AI, and designed with a human-first approach, we've created a platform where conversations flow naturally across language boundaries.

Today, people use Chorus to make friends across continents, learn new languages through immersion, conduct international business, and stay connected with loved ones abroad. We're just getting started.

As we expand to more languages, add more features, and reach more people, our core mission remains unchanged: to make the world a more connected place, one conversation at a time.
